Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock: How to Play!

Ready to throw down in an epic game that combines classic fun with a touch of sci-fi?


You have 10 attempts to beat the computer! Are you up for the challenge?


The Elements:

  • Rock (✊): A symbol of strength, rock crushes scissors and lizard.
  • Paper (✋): The all-covering element, paper smothers rock and disproves Spock's logic.
  • Scissors (✌️): Sharp and precise, scissors cut paper and decapitate lizard.
  • Lizard (️🦎): Quick and cunning, lizard devours paper and poisons Spock.
  • Spock (👽): Live long and prosper! Spock vaporizes rock and smashes scissors with his trusty Vulcan salute.

How to Win:


Simply throw down your hand gesture simultaneously with your opponent, following the dominance hierarchy:

  • Rock crushes Scissors and Lizard
  • Paper covers Rock and disproves Spock
  • Scissors cut Paper and decapitate Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper and poisons Spock
  • Spock vaporizes Rock and smashes Scissors

Ties:


If you both throw the same symbol, it's a tie! Play again to determine the victor.


Tips and Tricks:


  • Mix it up! Don't fall into a pattern, or your opponent might catch on.
  • Read your opponent. Are they rock-heavy? Throw some paper their way!
  • Embrace the Spock. This powerful move can surprise your opponent.
